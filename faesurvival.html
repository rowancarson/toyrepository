Avoidng capture by the Fae

Historically, fae have primarily kidnapped boy children due to the reduced evaluataion of girls in fae society

This mirrored the sexism within human society at the time. As a result, dressing young boys as girls used to be an effective way to thwart the fae.

This changed with the advent of Faeminism, and the passing of the Equal Sprites Amendment in Fairy Congress.

The seminal book on Faeminism, Our Bodies, Our Elves.

Instead of dressing your children in girl clothes, consider dressing them in bright red, and surrounding the cradle in salt.
